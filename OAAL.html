Organizing Asian-American Labor
A book focusing on the canned-salmon industry in the Pacific Northwest while also focusing on specifically the Asian Americans who labored in these facilities. The unions formed in the 1920-1930s demonstrate a special connection made for the first time between East Asian immigrants and Southeast Asian immigrants.

Friday, Chris. Organizing Asian-American Labor: The Pacific Coast Canned-Salmon Industry, 1870-1942. Temple University Press, 1994. Accessed April 6, 2020. $34.95, available on amazon.com.
