The Stone Junk

Stories of Malaysia and Neighbouring Lands

Howe, D. H., Sadlier, L.

The Stone Junk: Stories of Malaysia and Neighbouring Lands
This book is the reason that this collection exists. My father read this book to me when I was young. It was the strongest collection I had to my Malaysian culture and many lessons from those stories I have carried with me from childhood. Earlier this year when my father gave me this book, the memories associated with the book came rushing back and I realized that, while I might have felt cultural isolation growing up, there might be others who were in similar situations and I wanted to find those groups of people and understand their experiences under similar circumstance.

Howe, D. H., Sadlier, L. The Stone Junk: Stories of Malaysia and Neighbouring Lands, 1st edn. Oxford UK: Oxford University Press, 1984. Can be found on abebooks.com but the specific copy that is relevant to this collection is in my possession.
